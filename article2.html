We've been shortlisted!

Great news! We are excited to announce that following a rigorous nomination and application process, the judges at Routeone have shortlisted us in four categories: Operator Training Award; Large Bus Operator; The Best Use of Technology and Environment.

We are particularity pleased with our nomination for the Environment category. Whether it’s investing in gas buses, rainwater harvesting, solar panels on our depot roof or simply encouraging drivers to switch their engines off when they are stationary - we’re doing everything we can, no matter how small, to help the environment.

We're also really pleased with our shortlisting for The Best Use of Technology. This category shortlisting reflects the technology development, including our improved app and website where you can track your bus, see live bus times and plan your journey.

Well done to all our employees and their hard work which is reflected in the fact that we have again been shortlisted for so many awards. The winners will be announced in October, so until then, fingers crossed!
